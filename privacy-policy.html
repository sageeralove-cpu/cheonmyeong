1. About Us

천명 Cheonmyeong ("the App") is operated by Sage Era Pty Ltd, a company incorporated in Australia (ACN to be registered). Our support contact is sage.era.love@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use the App, you may optionally enter the following information to generate a Saju (Four Pillars) reading:

  • Date of birth (year, month, day)
  • Time of birth (optional, for Hour Pillar calculation)
  • City of birth (optional, for True Solar Time correction)
  • Gender (used in Daeun cycle direction calculation)
  • Name (used only for display purposes within the App)

2.2 How This Data Is Stored

  • All data you enter is stored exclusively in your device's browser localStorage or app-local storage.
  • We do not transmit this data to any server operated by Sage Era Pty Ltd.
  • Data remains on your device and is accessible only through the App on that device.
  • Clearing your browser/app data will permanently delete all stored information.

2.3 Subscription and Payment Data

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or the platform through which you downloaded the App. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card details. Subscription status is managed by Apple and synced to the App via Apple's in-app purchase APIs. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy for information about how Apple handles your payment data.

2.4 Referral Codes

If you share or receive a referral link, a randomly generated code is stored locally on your device. No personally identifiable information is attached to referral codes. Sage Era Pty Ltd does not maintain a server-side record of referral relationships.

2.5 Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App does not currently collect analytics or crash reporting data. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you enter is used solely to:

  • Calculate and display your Four Pillars (사주팔자) chart
  • Generate AI-assisted destiny readings and interpretations
  • Display compatibility analyses when you save multiple profiles
  • Apply True Solar Time corrections for more accurate Hour Pillar calculation

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the App's core features.

4. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

The AI oracle interpretations in this App are generated for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only. They are not medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. Interpretations are based on traditional Korean Saju (四柱八字) methodology and should not be relied upon as a basis for any decision. Always consult qualified professionals for matters requiring expert guidance.

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us at sage.era.love@gmail.com and we will take steps to remove that information.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:
